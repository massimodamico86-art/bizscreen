### The ≥90% gate math

- Denominator: passes + failures (skips are NOT counted — `gallery-tour.spec.js` may skip on certain envs per its skip-guard; we don't penalize skip-only files)
- Numerator: passes
- Pass rate: passes / (passes + failures)
- Gate: rate ≥ 0.90 (i.e., ≤1 failure across 18 tests — `17/18 = 94.4% PASS`; `16/18 = 88.9% FAIL`)
